Help Page

If you have any general inquiries about the survey-monkey website or need assistance using the website, you have navigated to the right place! Below in the drop-down menu you will find a few frequently asked questions by our users. Simply select the question you are interested in and you will be directed to the answer!

1. What is the Difference Between the Three Question Types?

The open ended question type takes answers in the form of text. The range question type takes number answers in the range specified. The multiple choice question type takes answers in the form of options where the number of options to choose from is between 2 and 5.

2. How do I Create a Survey?

To create a survey click the "Click to start creating a survey here" option. In the text field at the top of the page enter the name you want to call your survey. To create your first question select the question type from the drop down menu below where it states "Select a question type".

 If the question selected is an OpenEnded type:

 Click the "Create Question" button and enter your question in the text field that just got created. You have now successfully created an open ended question!

 If the question selected is a range question type:

 Click the "Create Question" button and enter your question in the text field that just got created. In the text field below enter the minimum and maximum range you would like the answers to  be. You have now successfully created a range question!

 If the question selected is a multiple choice type:

 Select the multiple choice option from the drop down menu. A text box will appear where you will enter the number of choices you would like your question to have in the text field under the  text prompt named "Number of MC answers:". Then select the "Create Question" button. Enter the question in the text field that just got created and enter the corresponding choices in the text  fields following

Once you have entered all the questions for your survey then click the "Create Survey Button". Your survey is now complete. To access the page to create a survey click here.

3. How do I Edit a Survey?

To edit a survey, you must be the survey creator. We do not give access to users to edit a survey they did not create. Click the “Click to edit a survey here” option which will take you to a list of surveys you have created. From the drop-down menu select the survey you would like to edit. Once the survey you would like to edit is selected in the drop-down menu select how you would like to edit the survey from the list of buttons below.

 Edit Survey: This will give you the option to edit the survey questions you have created.

 Close Survey: This will close the survey and stop users from entering answers.

 View Survey Results: This will show the survey results for the survey selected.

 Delete Survey: This will delete the entire survey selected.
